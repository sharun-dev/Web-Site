Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer? Contact our support team.

How do I list an item?

Simply click on "Sell / Upload" in the navigation, fill in your item details, and publish. Your listing will be live immediately.

How do I contact sellers?

Click on any product to view details, then click "Message Creator" to start a conversation with the seller.

What are the payment methods?

We support credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured and encrypted.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the seller's location. Most items ship within 2-5 business days.

Can I return an item?

Yes, we have a 30-day return policy for most items. Please review our full return policy for details.

Is TalentTrade secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data and transactions.
